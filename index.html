Career Story

Built to Build From Zero

Michael Lienert has built his career around a single consistent challenge: taking something that doesn't exist yet and making it real. He joined Los Angeles Football Club as one of the organization's first hires, before it had ever played a match, and helped the club sell through its entire premium inventory well ahead of Banc of California Stadium's opening.

That same build-from-scratch discipline carried through his work at SoFi Stadium with the Los Angeles Chargers, and into senior-level roles connected to the Detroit Tigers, Detroit Red Wings, and Chicago Fire FC.

Today, Michael is expanding his focus into real estate and broader business development initiatives, holding both a Michigan Real Estate License and a Michigan Life and Health Insurance License — credentials that reflect his interest in building a diversified, relationship-driven business platform. You can follow his ongoing work and writing on his Substack newsletter.
